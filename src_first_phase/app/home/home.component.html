WORLD OF PICKLEBALL

Pickleball was established in the summer of 1965. It has really taken off in the past 10 years with more play locations to play. Just recently it has taken off globally with places, players and tournaments all over.

This site will allow tournament directors to setup and run tournaments. Players to register for tournaments and also be able to house a profile where they can share information about themselves from a pickleball perspective and upload photos to share for all.

Local clubs can also use this site as a portal to house information about their club and use it to share information to their own members and also players outside.

The shopping link will allow vendors big and small to use this site as a pass through to their site. This will allow a one stop location for players to access all things Pickleball.

The clinics will give players an idea on clinics that are being offered and they can contact and get information on what is out there.
